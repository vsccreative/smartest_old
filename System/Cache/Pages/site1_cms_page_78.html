          Allan Dolan

          You are in: Home >> Directors & Management >> Allan Dolan

          Chairman & Executive Director

          Also Managing Director of Clarity Capital Management (part of the Clarity Group, Savannah's sponsor and largest investor), Allan has a particular focus on the minerals industry. He has specialised in mineral resource acquisition and development since the mid 1990's and is the founder and a significant shareholder of African Minerals Limited (formerly Sierra Leone Diamond Company) which listed on the London Stock Exchange's AIM Market in early 2005, raising £21 million on listing.

          Allan has a background in both business and corporate law.  As partner at Burnet Duckworth and Palmer, one of Canada's most successful corporate and resource law firms www.bdplaw.com, he specialised in international resource and finance transactions.  Allan brings considerable strategic, financing and leadership skills, as well as strong relationships within the investor community.
